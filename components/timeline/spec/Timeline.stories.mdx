# Timeline

A timeline of events. An event must have an action, a date (& time) and a description.
It may also have an actor (a person or system) associated with it.

### Interaction principles
Use where information is ordered by time and unfiltered. If the information isnâ€™t displayed in time order or needs to be filtered, consider another approach.

### Content principles
Times and dates should follow GDS patterns on date and time format

Secondary titles aren't mandatory.
